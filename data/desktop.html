Welcome to XevoLve

This is a demo/playground for Bind.JS Widgets, here you can:

  • Add: Widgets you want to Your desktop.
  • Modify: existing Widgets to Your needs.
  • Create: Your own hilarious/or useful Widgets.

Compatibility:

  • FireFox: 100% - All what's coded, works (Clock starts to run faster after a while!?).
  • IE 11: 99% - Meny-plugin bugs, and Clock does not move.
  • Chrome: 60% - CodePanel/Widgets has some issues.
  • Other: Not tested.

Start by adding widgets to your desktop from the left side menu.

What's going on?

Main reason for this is to test the capabilities of Bind.JS and also combine lot's of other useful scripts like:

  • Bootstrap: http://getbootstrap.com/
  • HeadJs: http://headjs.com/
  • Tmpl.js: https://github.com/blueimp/javascript-templates
  • Meny: http://lab.hakim.se/meny/
  • Nifty Modal: http://tympanus.net/Development/ModalWindowEffects/
  • Code Mirror: http://codemirror.net/

Clearing local storage?

This will remove all settings saved by this site to your browsers local storage.

This includes:

  • My widgets: Clear all custom widgets and reset My widgets to default state.
  • Desktop: Remove all widgets from your desktop and return it to the default state.
